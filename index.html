DEMYSTIFYING DATA SCIENCE

Relentless in focus & discipline for facilitating practical data science and artificial intelligence applications, solutions, and infrastructures through supporting legitimate products, principles and people

WHAT WE DO

To bring out the latent potential and power of possibility through programs and infrastructure for education in data science and artificial intelligence

IDSOL Programs

The International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL) helps students and young people learn about data science and artificial intelligence. IDSOL approves educational materials and certifies courses around the world.

IDSOL Gatherings

The International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL) manages conferences and competitions, which help people who study and work in data sciences and artificial intelligence to share ideas and work together.

IDSOL Working Groups

The International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL) helps set up groups of academic, industry, and regulatory experts for setting rules and standards concerning data science and artificial intelligence.

IDSOL Infrastructure

The International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL) builds systems that help students and practitioners develop responsible data science and artificial intelligence applications, solutions, and infrastructures.

WHY NOW

AI is a powerful technology, but its true power lies in its ability to amplify human efforts in tackling global challenges, many of which are encapsulated in the Sustainable Development Goals

IDSOL Foundation (International Data Science Olympiad)

IDSOL Foundation is an EU non-profit elevating intelligence and competence in data science, including machine learning and artificial intelligence, and promoting practical solutions to foster a global community of practitioners and to empower young people through education so that they make informed career choices.
