International Data Science Olympiad Seeks Country Partners for IDSOL 2024 in Amsterdam

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE14 February 2024

The International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL), a leading global competition dedicated to innovation and education in data science and artificial intelligence (AI), is actively seeking country partners to represent and participate in the upcoming IDSOL 2024. The 2024 International Data Science Olympiad are scheduled to take place in the vibrant city of Amsterdam, bringing together the brightest minds from around the world.

IDSOL invites educational institutions, technology associations, and industry leaders to become country partners, tasked with forming and nominating a delegation to represent their nation at this prestigious event. This partnership offers a unique opportunity to showcase local talent on an international stage and contribute to the global blockchain community.

Opportunity for Partners:
Country partners will play a crucial role by:

  • Identifying and assembling a team of top students and young professionals with a passion for data science and AI.
  • Providing these teams with the resources and support needed to develop their innovative projects.
  • Nominating the teams to compete in the IDSOL 2024 finals.

Benefits of Partnership:
Country partners will benefit through:

  • Enhancing your organization’s visibility and prestige in the data science and AI tech sectors.
  • Fostering innovation and talent development within your country.
  • Building and strengthening international networks with other leaders in the data science and AI community.

Event Details:
More information shall be released in the coming months:

  • Event: 2024 International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL 2024)
  • Location: Amsterdam, The Netherlands, EU
  • Date: Week of 11 November 2024

Interested parties are encouraged to express their interest and request more information about becoming a country partner by contacting the IDSOL 2024 Organizing Committee here

About the International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL):
The International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL) is a competition that invites all students and recent graduates (not just in tech) to propose interesting ideas for using data and sources from the real world, from discovering insights gleaned from open data to novel applications of current and emerging machine learning capabilities.

About IDSOL 2024:
The 2024 International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL 2024) is hosted in the Randstad region of the Netherlands in mid-November 2024, with the final event hosted in the centre of Amsterdam. If you are a current or recent student, submit a project according to the guidelines.
