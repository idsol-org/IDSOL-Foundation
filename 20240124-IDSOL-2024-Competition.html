International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL) Season 2024 Launches on UN International Day of Education

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE24 January 2024

In a fitting tribute to the transformative power of education, the International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL) announces the commencement of its 2024 season on this year’s International Day of Education. This global event underscores the critical role of educational initiatives in advancing technology and innovation worldwide.

The 2024 season of the IDSOL invites students and young professionals from around the globe to engage in the challenge of harnessing data sciences and artificial intelligence (AI) for creative and practical solutions to real-world problems. The event aims to foster a deeper understanding of data science and AI along with its potential applications, while also promoting a spirit of international cooperation and knowledge exchange.

Season Highlights:

  • Participants will compete by developing original data science and AI solutions across various sectors, including finance, healthcare, and sustainability.
  • The season will culminate in the prestigious IDSOL 2024 finals, to be held in Amsterdam, where finalists will present their projects to a panel of international experts.

Empowering the Next Generation:
Launching the season on the International Day of Education highlights IDSOL’s commitment to educational excellence and its role in empowering the next generation of technology leaders. IDSOL not only challenges participants to think critically and innovatively but also provides a platform for exceptional educational and networking opportunities.

Call to Action:
Educational institutions, technology enthusiasts, and students worldwide are encouraged to participate in this enriching experience. Registration details and competition guidelines are available on the IDSOL 2024 official website.

For further information about IDSOL 2024 and how to participate, please visit 2024.idsol.org.

About the International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL):
The International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL) is a competition that invites all students and recent graduates (not just in tech) to propose interesting ideas for using data and sources from the real world, from discovering insights gleaned from open data to novel applications of current and emerging machine learning capabilities.

About IDSOL 2024:
The 2024 International Data Science Olympiad (IDSOL 2024) is hosted in the Randstad region of the Netherlands in mid-November 2024, with the final event hosted in the centre of Amsterdam. If you are a current or recent student, submit a project according to the guidelines.
